# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. This project helps identify key factors affecting attrition using KPI cards, charts, slicers, and business insights.

---

## Overview

This dashboard was created to understand employee attrition patterns and support HR decision-making through visual analysis. It highlights important workforce metrics such as employee count, attrition rate, age distribution, salary trends, overtime impact, and department-wise attrition.

## Features

- KPI cards for:
  - Total Employees
  - Total Attrition
  - Attrition Rate
  - Average Age
  - Average Monthly Income

- Visual analysis of attrition by:
  - Department
  - Job Role
  - Age Group
  - OverTime
  - Salary Slab

- Interactive slicers for:
  - Department
  - Job Role
  - Gender
  - Marital Status

- Key insights section for quick business understanding

## Dashboard KPIs

The dashboard includes the following key metrics:

- **Total Employees:** 1480
- **Total Attrition:** 238
- **Attrition Rate:** 16.08%
- **Average Age:** 36.92
- **Average Monthly Income:** 6.50K

---

## Key Insights

- Research & Development has the highest employee count and notable attrition.
- Employees aged **26–35** show the highest attrition.
- Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.
- Most attrition comes from lower salary slabs.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze employee attrition patterns and identify workforce trends that may help HR teams improve retention and decision-making.
